Hershey vector fonts (.jhf)

The Hershey Fonts were originally created by Dr. A. V. Hershey while working at
the U.S. National Bureau of Standards. The .jhf vector format and this data were
prepared and distributed by James Hurt. The fonts are in the public domain; the
following acknowledgement accompanies their use:

    This distribution includes the Hershey Fonts.
    Created by Dr. A. V. Hershey (U.S. National Bureau of Standards),
    vector format by James Hurt.

Used here only to draw single-stroke text with a pen.
